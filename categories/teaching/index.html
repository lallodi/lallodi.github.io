Courses Offensive Computer Security (2IC80) This is an introductory course on hacking, with a focus on computer networks and software. The full course will revolve around the idea that (most) mechanisms can be illegitimately misused to obtain (some level of) a desired behaviour; this is the very essence of computer security.
